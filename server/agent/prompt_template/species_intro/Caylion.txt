  - Effect: The economic converter on a colony with a Seedling Token runs TWICE during the Economy Phase. (e.g., if it produces 1 Green, it now produces 2 Green). Both runs of the converter are still simultaneous with all other converters.
  - Upgrading: The cost to flip (upgrade) a colony with a token is not doubled, and the token stays after filp (upgrade). The new side also produces double. 
  - Trading: If you trade a colony with a token to another player, the token stays on it.
